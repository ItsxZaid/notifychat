## ðŸš€ The Core Idea

Why does this exist? Because managing notifications across multiple platforms is a mess.

Manually sending alerts or updates to clients on both WhatsApp and Telegram is repetitive, error-prone, and hard to scale. **NotifyChat** automates this entire process. It gives you a single, reliable hub to manage and send all your notifications without the pain.
